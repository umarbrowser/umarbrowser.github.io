What is GSU Token

The GSU Token is an innovative payment network and a novel form of currency designed specifically for Gombe State University.

Implement the designed cryptocurrency on the Solana Blockchain into the university's financial infrastructure for real-world transactions, such as purchasing goods and services from university-affiliated vendors, and making payments for tuition and fees, etc.

Get Started With GSU Token

Start learning about GSU Token with interactive tutorials. It’s fun, easy, and takes only a few minutes!

Installation

Begin by downloading and installing the Phantom Wallet app on your preferred mobile device. You can find it on the respective app stores for your operating system.

Create Your Wallet

Open the Phantom Wallet app and follow the prompts to create a new wallet. This typically involves generating a secure passphrase or seed phrase that serves as the key to your wallet.

Managing Transactions

Once your wallet is set up, you can use it to send, receive, and manage your cryptocurrency holdings.
