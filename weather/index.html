Climate data

We are a climate data and analytics company

We use remote sensing and computer vision to track carbon emissions from manufacturing, agriculture and burning of fossil fuel. We monitor human activities that trigger climate change on the earth surface and changes on the environment which are related to the effect of climate change, and climate change influenced disasters.

  • Desert encroachment
  • Rising of sea level
