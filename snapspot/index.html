SnapSpot – Data Deletion Policy

Effective Date: 15 February 2025

SnapSpot is designed to store your saved locations, images, and titles locally on your device. Your data is never uploaded to a server, and we do not collect or share your personal information.

How to Delete Your Data:

1. Delete Saved Locations Manually:

  • Open the SnapSpot app.
  • Go to your saved locations list.
  • Select the location you want to remove and press the delete button.

2. Delete All Data by Uninstalling the App:

  • Uninstalling SnapSpot will permanently remove all stored locations and images.
